At the end of every great day at Epcot in Walt Disney World is the award-winning fireworks, laser, and fountain show IllumiNations: Reflections of Earth. It started in 1999 to celebrate the new millenuium but proved so popular that it continues to this day. The soundtrack is wonderful, and it great for ambient music.
